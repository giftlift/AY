Adrienne Covington (she/her) – Licensed Massage Therapist

Adrienne has been working as an LMT since 2017 - receiving her training from Hocking College in 2016. Offering therapeutic bodywork aimed to balanced the body structurally while also initiating the opportunity for reconnection of the mind & body. Adrienne’s focus is in therapeutic modalities such as myofascial release, trigger point therapy + deep tissue. Adrienne promotes overall health and wellness to a variety of clients. Through her practice, Adrienne creates a welcoming and recharging space for healing to take place- encouraging clients to be apart of their own healing process.
